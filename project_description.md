This project tests the **contrarian signal hypothesis** in currency trading: whether *retail sentiment extremes* predict price direction.

A *sentiment extreme* occurs when a large (>70%) group of Forex (FX) retail traders are positioned in a certain direction, either *long* (hoping for prices to rise) or *short* (hoping for prices to fall). 

---

**TL/DR:**

- Built a research pipeline that merges multi-year retail FX sentiment snapshots with hourly FX market data, aligns timestamps across timezones, computes trading-bar forward returns, and evaluates contrarian predictive signals across multiple horizons and pair groups.
